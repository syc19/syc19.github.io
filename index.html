About Me

#

I'm a postdoc at the Chicago Booth School of Business, interested in judgment and choice under uncertainty. #

My research focuses on moral judgment and on issues of public policy. In both projects I take an inferential approach, examining the ways that people “go beyond the information given” to solve the task at hand.
